About SS Cliiniic

ASTHMA, ALLERGY, CHEST DISEASES, TB, INFERTILITY & WOMEN CARE CENTRE.

SS clinic is running from 2007 in the town Viluppuram. This clinic delivers services for asthma, allergy, TB disorders and also specially for infertility patients at affordable cost.

The most success of the centre is more than 250 couples had conceived by IUI alone and proper counselling .

Dr. R.SUDHAKAR M.B.B.S, DTCD

Dr Sudhakar has completed MBBS at Government Mohan Kumaramangalam Medical College hospital and he passed as a distinction holder and has received "Best student award" by IMA Salem in year 2002. He has completed Post graduate diploma at Institute of thoracic medicine, Chetpet, affliated to madras medical College and was a University rank holder. He has a vast experience in the field of TB for 15 years and has treated more than 5000 TB patients. He also acts as District TB officer of the viluppuram district. He was awarded "The Best Doctor" award by health department of Govt. Health dept of Govt of Tamilnadu. He is also one of the state level trainer for medical officers and paramedical staffs on TB management. Appreciated by Honourable Chief Minister of Tamilnadu for good work in Controlling Tuberculosis.
